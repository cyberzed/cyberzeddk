about

2021-05-06 1 min read

As a kid I never thought I should be working in the IT industry, mostly because it wasn’t that big back in the 80’s. The dream of dreams was becoming a biologist, but at some point in my life it all changed. I meet a few computer geeks and it all spiraled towards becoming a developer.

Over the years I’ve owned everything from a Commodore C64 to a PC with quite a few years in the Amiga space…way longer than many of my fellow geeks.

Spare time is spent in the world of beers by brewing and judging, but a healthy interest like weightlifting comes in hand to burn those extra calories from the beers.

More recently I’ve become quite fund of getting a wide range of security tools under my skin in the form of completing walkthrough and challenge rooms on TryHackMe.
